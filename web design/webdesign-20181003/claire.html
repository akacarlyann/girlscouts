Community Service

Feminine Products Drive:

This drive will be on ... We will be collecting feminine products to girls in Ghana who really need them. Most girls in Ghana can not afford feminine products. Instead they have to find other non-efficient, sometimes unsanitary ways to take care of the issue. One hundred percent of the items collected will go to the girls in need.

Current Events

On September 26, 2018, Francis H. Arnold was awarded The Noble Peace Prize in Chemistry. She recieved this award for her work on conducting the directed evolution of enzymes. She became the fifth woman to recieve this award. Before recieving the award, she became an American profesor of chemical engineering, bioengineering, and biochemistry at the California Institute of Technology in Pasedena. She split the award with George P. Smith and Gregory P. Winter. The award was worth close to $1 million dollars. (The New York Times)
